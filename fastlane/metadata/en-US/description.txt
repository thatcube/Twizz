Twizz is a fast, native Apple TV player for Twitch — free and open source.

• Native tvOS experience built for the big screen
• Live Twitch chat with 7TV, BTTV, and FFZ emote support
• Combine Twitch and YouTube live chat in a single view
• Incoming raid detection so you can follow the action
• Deep chat appearance customization
• Dark, Light, and OLED themes
